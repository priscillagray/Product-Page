It all started with drinking coffee just because my older siblings did. I didn't understand anything except it's what the "cool" kids were doing. By the time I hit college coffee became a necessity for caffeine, comfort, and community. When I moved to Atlanta and worked at a coffee shop, I began to understand the art of it. I started to fall in love with the aroma, taste, the science and method behind it all. I wanted to learn as much as I could. Coffee is not just a caffeinated drink to me. Coffee represents an experience with something I love and with people I love.

Now it is your turn to make an experience.
